Terms and Conditions

1. General Information

Ridex Leather specializes in premium leather products, primarily related to equestrian gear, including horse saddles, bridles, harnesses, and other leather accessories.

2. Ordering and Payment
  • All orders must be placed through our official website.
  • Accepted payment methods: Credit/Debit Cards, PayPal, etc.
  • Prices are subject to change without prior notice.
  • International orders may be subject to VAT and customs duties, payable by the buyer.
3. Shipping and Delivery
  • We offer domestic and international shipping.
  • International shipping includes additional delivery charges and VAT.
  • We do not currently ship to Peru, or Chile unless extra charges are agreed upon.
  • Customers must provide an accurate shipping address.
4. Refund Products
  • Products must be returned in new, unused condition. Standard products are eligible for return within 30 days, while specific products require returns within 10 days.
  • Customers are responsible for return shipping, and original shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • A return label will be emailed (usable for multiple items), and a $9.99 fee is deducted from the refund.
  • Refunds are processed within 3-7 business days upon receipt of the return, with credits applied to the original payment method.
  • If returned items are not in new condition, a 15% restocking fee may apply, or the return may be refused. Defective items will be replaced at no additional cost.
5. Customer Support & Dispute Resolution

If you are dissatisfied with a product, please contact us before raising a dispute. Our team will respond within 24 hours.
